About

R.U. Hacking? is a student-led society at the University of Reading. We look forward to welcoming approximately 100 students to our 24-hour student hackathon for a fun weekend of code, collaboration, and creativity.

At all R.U. Hacking? events, all attendees (including, but not limited to, sponsors, venue staff, and committee members) are expected to abide by the MLH Code of Conduct.
